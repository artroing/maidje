MAIDJE MEERGANS

Documentary Photography

Portfolio on request

Maidje Meergans, 1991, is a freelance photographer living in Berlin. After studying Design at Kunsthochschule Weißensee, she graduated from Ostkreuzschule für Fotografie in 2018. There she studied under the photographers Ute Mahler, Werner Mahler, Sibylle Fendt and Prof. Linn Schroeder.

With her graduation project ‚the story of ernst‘ about the Michelin starred restaurant in Berlin, she shifted the focus of her future practice to long-term projects. Documentary photography with an emphasis on social and environmental topics is now at the heart of her photographic work.

Since 2019 she is part of aff Galerie in Berlin.
