MAIDJE MEERGANS

Documentary Photography

Portfolio on request

Maidje Meergans, born 1991 in Berlin, lives and works as a photographer in Berlin. After studying Design at Kunsthochschule Berlin Weißensee, she changed her field in 2015 to start at Ostkreuzschule für Fotografie, where she studied under Ute Mahler, Werner Mahler, Sibylle Fendt and Prof. Linn Schroeder. She was part of the class of 2018. Since 2019 she is part of aff Galerie, Berlin.
